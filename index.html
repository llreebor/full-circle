New York City Early intervention Program

Enabling families to achieve their potential through early diagnosis and outstanding, innovative, interdisciplinary care, our vision is to transform lives as a leader in pioneering, holistic, and person-centered care.

Our interdisciplinary team delivers comprehensive support by incorporating applied behavior analysis (ABA), speech and occupational therapy, physical therapy, nursing, audiological and multidisciplinary evaluations to support children and their families throughout all stages of development and growth.
